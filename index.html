Hey Lovey! I hope that you're excited to be reading this--I had so much fun putting this little website together for you! I only hope it can do a better job at representing our relationship than my shitty handwriting.

Although this August we'll be apart 💔, August has always been such a fun and exciting time with you! There's such an air of excitement and because school is only just starting, we get to hang out a lot and do fun things (like the beach trip in the collage below😛). I think its the perfect month for us to begin this journey in, its always been a month full of change and excitement as we look to the new year! After all, we first met duing August.

But maybe its exactly that that makes it just so scary though--there's this sense that so much is up in the air and everything is subject to change, including our relationship. I've been thinking a lot about, and I promise that this is related, what it is exactly that makes romantic love so different from the love we share with our friends. Like sure I'll always love you through your ups and downs and the changes your life may experience, but so will your friends. And maybe this is the monogamous programming of my brain speaking, but I think at least one of the things that makes them so different, is to have chosen someone who chose you back. I am so fortunate to be loved by someone who makes me feel chosen and so special for it. I just hope that I can express to you how true this is from me too, how irreplaceable you are to me.

In every other world, country, or time zone, I choose you.
